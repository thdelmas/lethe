# LETHE — Privacy Policy

**Last updated:** 2026-04-11
**Applies to:** LETHE v1.0.0+

---

## What LETHE is

LETHE is a privacy-hardened Android overlay built on LineageOS. It includes an AI agent
powered by language models that runs as a system service. This policy explains what data
LETHE collects, where it goes, and what you control.

## AI-generated content

LETHE's responses are generated by artificial intelligence (language models). They are
not written by a human. AI-generated messages are labeled in the chat interface.

Responses may be inaccurate, incomplete, or outdated. Do not rely on LETHE for medical,
legal, or financial advice. Verify anything that matters.

## Data collection

### What LETHE does NOT collect

- No telemetry or analytics
- No usage tracking or behavioral profiling
- No advertising identifiers
- No data sent to OSmosis, LETHE developers, or any third party by default
- No cross-device communication or synchronization

### What stays on your device

- All conversations (ephemeral by default — erased on reboot in burner mode)
- Device state (battery, connectivity, Tor status)
- Settings and provider configuration
- Voice input (processed locally when using local STT models)

### What may leave your device

When you configure a **cloud AI provider** (Anthropic, OpenRouter, or a custom endpoint),
your conversation messages are sent to that provider's servers for processing. This only
happens when:

1. **You explicitly configure** a cloud provider by entering an API key
2. **You send a message** in the chat interface
3. **The local model is unavailable** and fallback to cloud is enabled

LETHE shows a consent notice the first time you configure a cloud provider.

## Cloud providers

| Provider | What they receive | Their privacy policy |
|---|---|---|
| **Local (on-device)** | Nothing leaves the device | N/A |
| **Anthropic** | Your conversation messages | https://www.anthropic.com/privacy |
| **OpenRouter** | Your conversation messages (routed to model providers) | https://openrouter.ai/privacy |
| **Custom endpoint** | Your conversation messages | Depends on your endpoint |

LETHE does not control how cloud providers handle your data after transmission.
Review each provider's privacy policy before configuring their service.

## Data retention

- **Burner mode (default):** All user data, conversations, and settings are wiped on
  every reboot. Nothing persists.
- **Burner mode disabled:** Conversations are stored in the app's local storage.
  They are never sent anywhere unless you use a cloud provider.
- **Cloud providers:** Retention depends on the provider's policy. LETHE does not
  cache or re-send previous conversations after a session ends.

## Network traffic

- All network traffic is routed through **Tor** by default (transparent proxy, not a toggle)
- DNS queries are resolved through Tor
- UDP is blocked for user apps (Tor is TCP-only)
- The Tor circuit is isolated per app to prevent cross-app correlation
- No traffic bypasses the Tor firewall rules

## User rights

You have full control over your data:

- **Access:** All data is on your device. There is no remote database.
- **Deletion:** Reboot in burner mode, or factory reset. Data is gone.
- **Portability:** Your data is local files on an Android device.
- **Provider choice:** Switch between local and cloud at any time in Settings.
- **Consent withdrawal:** Remove your API key to stop cloud processing immediately.

## Device identity

LETHE rotates device identifiers by default:

- MAC address randomization
- Android ID rotation
- Device serial number spoofing

These rotations happen on every reboot in burner mode.

## Children

LETHE is not designed for or directed at children under 13. The AI agent does not
implement age-gating, parental controls, or content filtering beyond its system-level
behavioral boundaries.
