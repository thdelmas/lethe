# LETHE Funding Map — Alternatives to NLnet NGI Zero Commons

*Compiled 2026-04-14. Single-developer FOSS project, France-based. Primary application (NLnet NGI0 Commons) due 2026-06-01.*

Ranked by realistic fit for a first-time individual French applicant over the next 6-12 months.

> **Verification note:** Entry #2 (NGI Zero Core) is listed as active here, but earlier NLnet research indicated the 8th and final Core call closed 2024-10-01. Double-check before pursuing — may be outdated.

---

### 1. Sovereign Tech Fund (Germany) — Fit 5/5
- **URL:** https://www.sovereigntech.fund/
- **Scope:** Critical open digital infrastructure. Funds maintenance, security hardening, improvements. Explicitly includes operating-system-layer components.
- **Size:** EUR 50k–500k typical; contracts, not grants.
- **Deadline:** Rolling (Bug Resilience + Contract programs).
- **Eligibility:** Individuals OK via contractor model; international (non-German) projects welcome.
- **Gotcha:** Contract/procurement vehicle, so invoices + VAT matter. Compatible with NLnet.

### 2. NGI Zero Core (sister call to Commons) — Fit 5/5 ⚠️ verify status
- **URL:** https://nlnet.nl/core/
- **Scope:** Trustworthy open internet tech — privacy, decentralisation, security. LETHE fits squarely.
- **Size:** EUR 5k–50k (occasionally higher).
- **Deadline:** Previously rolling; earlier research indicates 8th/final call closed 2024-10-01.
- **Eligibility:** Individuals, EU + associated.
- **Gotcha:** Same foundation as Commons — NLnet discourages parallel applications on same topic; sequence them, don't stack.

### 3. NGI TALER / NGI Mobifree — Fit 4/5
- **URL:** https://www.mobifree.org/ ; https://taler-systems.com/ngi/
- **Scope:** Mobifree is explicitly a free-software mobile ecosystem NGI cascade — privacy-respecting mobile OS work is in-scope.
- **Size:** EUR 5k–60k per sub-grant.
- **Deadline:** Check open calls; Mobifree had a 2025 round, expect 2026 follow-up.
- **Eligibility:** EU + associated; individuals OK.
- **Gotcha:** Very direct fit — prioritise watching this. Cascade funding = lighter paperwork than Horizon direct.

### 4. Prototype Fund (DE, via Open Knowledge Foundation) — Fit 4/5
- **URL:** https://prototypefund.de/
- **Scope:** Public-interest FOSS prototypes, 6-month fellowships.
- **Size:** EUR 47,500 fixed.
- **Deadline:** Typically 2 rounds/year; next ~autumn 2026.
- **Eligibility:** **German residents only** historically — but Prototype Fund EU pilot (via Sovereign Tech Agency) is being set up. Watch for EU-wide round.
- **Gotcha:** Currently DE-only; EU variant is the realistic lane for Théophile.

### 5. Mozilla Technology Fund — Fit 4/5
- **URL:** https://foundation.mozilla.org/en/what-we-fund/awards/mozilla-technology-fund-mtf/
- **Scope:** Thematic yearly call (past: AI transparency, privacy/anti-tracking). Privacy-hardened mobile OS could fit a future theme.
- **Size:** Up to USD 100k.
- **Deadline:** Annual, theme-dependent; watch Q1 each year.
- **Eligibility:** Global; individuals via fiscal host OK.
- **Gotcha:** Theme must align — not every year will fit. MOSS (separate) is smaller and rolling.

### 6. Bpifrance — Bourse French Tech Émergence / Aide au Développement Deeptech — Fit 3/5
- **URL:** https://www.bpifrance.fr/
- **Scope:** Innovation funding. FOSS is fundable but Bpifrance strongly prefers a legal entity (SAS/association).
- **Size:** EUR 30k–90k (Bourse Émergence).
- **Deadline:** Rolling.
- **Eligibility:** **Requires French legal entity** — auto-entrepreneur usually excluded for Bourse Émergence; association loi 1901 works.
- **Gotcha:** Heavy French admin; need to incorporate first.

### 7. ADEME — Économie Circulaire / Allongement durée de vie des produits — Fit 3/5
- **URL:** https://agirpourlatransition.ademe.fr/
- **Scope:** E-waste reduction, product-lifespan extension. LETHE's "resurrect 2012 devices" story matches.
- **Size:** EUR 20k–200k.
- **Deadline:** Periodic AAP (appels à projets); check 2026 calendar.
- **Eligibility:** Legal entity required (association works).
- **Gotcha:** Angle as e-waste project, not as security project. Software-only projects sometimes rejected — frame as device-reuse enabler.

### 8. Fondation de France / Fondation Free — Fit 3/5
- **URL:** https://www.fondationdefrance.org/ ; https://fondation.iliad.fr/
- **Scope:** General/digital-inclusion grants. Fondation Free (Iliad) funds digital access projects.
- **Size:** EUR 5k–50k.
- **Deadline:** Rolling / annual.
- **Eligibility:** French associations preferred.
- **Gotcha:** Need association wrapper; framing must emphasise social impact over tech.

### 9. GitHub Sponsors + Open Collective — Fit 4/5 (not a grant, but pipeline-critical)
- **URL:** https://github.com/sponsors ; https://opencollective.com/
- **Scope:** Recurring individual + corporate sponsorship for FOSS maintainers.
- **Size:** EUR 0–unbounded; realistic solo maintainer EUR 100–2000/month after launch.
- **Deadline:** N/A.
- **Eligibility:** Individuals welcome; GitHub Sponsors France is live.
- **Gotcha:** Needs active audience — couples with the awesome-* release com plan.

### 10. Open Technology Fund — Internet Freedom Fund — Fit 3/5
- **URL:** https://www.opentech.fund/funds/internet-freedom-fund/
- **Scope:** Tools that counter authoritarian surveillance/censorship. Privacy mobile OS fits if framed for at-risk users.
- **Size:** USD 10k–900k (typ. 50k–300k).
- **Deadline:** Rolling.
- **Eligibility:** EU projects ARE eligible. Individuals via fiscal sponsor.
- **Gotcha:** US State Dept funding — some EU FOSS projects decline on principle; also politically volatile (2025 funding disruptions). Verify current status before applying.

### 11. Shuttleworth Foundation Fellowship — Fit 3/5
- **URL:** https://www.shuttleworthfoundation.org/fellowship/
- **Scope:** Open-everything fellowships for individual change-makers.
- **Size:** ~USD 275k/year (salary + 10x match on personal investment).
- **Deadline:** Rolling, 2 rounds/year.
- **Eligibility:** Global, individuals — ideal structure for Théophile.
- **Gotcha:** Highly selective (~1% acceptance); requires compelling personal narrative — the origin-story memo helps.

### 12. Reset Tech — Fit 2/5
- **URL:** https://www.reset.tech/
- **Scope:** Mostly policy/research on platform power. Weak fit for a ROM, but privacy-infra angle possible.
- **Size:** Varies.
- **Deadline:** By invitation / targeted calls.
- **Eligibility:** EU OK.
- **Gotcha:** Rarely funds code directly. Low priority.

### 13. Internet Society Foundation — BOLT / Research grants — Fit 2/5
- **URL:** https://www.isocfoundation.org/
- **Scope:** Internet access, security, trust. Mobile OS is tangential.
- **Size:** USD 10k–100k.
- **Deadline:** Annual cycles.
- **Eligibility:** Global; fiscal host recommended.
- **Gotcha:** Prefers network-layer / access projects over endpoint OS.

### 14. Horizon Europe Cascade Funding (various FSTP calls) — Fit 3/5
- **URL:** https://ngi.eu/opencalls/ ; https://fstp-dashboard.eu/
- **Scope:** Many NGI/Horizon projects re-grant to SMEs & individuals. NGI Search, NGI Sargasso (transatlantic), NGI Enrichers each run sub-calls.
- **Size:** EUR 5k–150k.
- **Deadline:** Rolling; check fstp-dashboard monthly.
- **Eligibility:** EU + associated; individuals sometimes OK via cascade.
- **Gotcha:** Each cascade has its own theme — LETHE fits Mobifree-type calls best. Aggregate dashboard is the efficient watch point.

### 15. LFX Mentorship (Linux Foundation) — Fit 2/5
- **URL:** https://lfx.linuxfoundation.org/tools/mentorship/
- **Scope:** Paid mentorship on LF-hosted projects. LineageOS isn't LF-hosted, so indirect only.
- **Size:** USD 3k–6.6k stipends.
- **Eligibility:** Individuals; Théophile would apply as mentee, not project lead.
- **Gotcha:** Not a project-funding lane — listed for completeness; skip unless onboarding help is needed.

---

## Skipped (explicitly weak fit)
- **EA Funds / Long-Term Future Fund / SFF** — AI-safety framing is thin for a FOSS mobile OS; guardian agent is a feature, not the project.
- **Digital Freedom Fund** — funds strategic litigation, not software.
- **Ford Foundation Digital Rights** — large orgs only, no individual pipeline.
- **GNOME/KDE mentorship** — irrelevant to Android ROM.
- **Scaleway Startup Program** — equity/credits, not FOSS grants.

## Funding Roadmap — 12-Month Plan

Goal: a **diversified, non-blocking** funding pipeline. No single source
funds LETHE; no single rejection kills it. Target total raised in 12
months: **€60–120k** across 2–3 sources.

### Phase 0 — Now → 2026-05-04 (pre-release, pre-NLnet deadline)

**Focus: finish the NLnet submission; launch v1.0.0 on May 4.**

- [ ] Finish NLnet NGI0 Commons proposal (see `lethe-nlnet-draft.md`)
- [ ] Await NLnet eligibility reply (sent 2026-04-14 via web form)
- [ ] Decide legal structure *before* incorporating anything:
      individual applicant works for NLnet + STF + Shuttleworth + GitHub
      Sponsors + OTF; incorporation only needed for ADEME / Bpifrance /
      Fondation Free. So: do NOT incorporate until Phase 3.
- [ ] Launch LETHE v1.0.0 (May 4, 2026) → unlocks Phase 1 credibility

**Decision gate 2026-05-04:** did v1.0.0 ship on time?
- Yes → continue Phase 1 on schedule.
- No → delay STF application 4–6 weeks; a working release is the
  single strongest input to every subsequent funder.

### Phase 1 — 2026-05-04 → 2026-06-30 (submit & diversify)

**Focus: submit NLnet; open second and third funding tracks.**

- [ ] 2026-06-01 12:00 CEST — **Submit NLnet NGI0 Commons** (hard deadline)
- [ ] Launch GitHub Sponsors + Open Collective pages (same day as v1.0.0
      announce; couples with the awesome-* release com plan)

      conflict with NLnet; STF explicitly welcomes parallel funding)

- NLnet reviews take 4–8 weeks; expect reply by late July / August.

### Phase 2 — 2026-07-01 → 2026-09-30 (STF + Mobifree)

**Focus: add the contract-scale funder and catch cascade calls.**

- [ ] Submit Sovereign Tech Fund proposal
- [ ] Monitor NGI Mobifree open calls monthly; apply when next round opens
- [ ] Monitor Horizon Europe FSTP dashboard monthly
- [ ] Continue building sponsor base on GitHub Sponsors / Open Collective
      (target: €500/month by Sept)

**Decision gate 2026-09-30:** how many sources have accepted / replied?
- **0 funded**: re-evaluate scope. Is the proposal too broad?
  Consider narrowing to one WP (e.g. just Burner Mode) and resubmitting.
- **1 funded (likely NLnet)**: on-track. Continue Phase 3 on schedule.
- **2+ funded**: do NOT incorporate yet — handle via individual tax
  regime until volume justifies the admin burden.

### Phase 3 — 2026-10-01 → 2027-04-14 (French lanes, fellowship shots)

**Focus: unlock FR-specific grants; take long-shot fellowship swings.**

- [ ] Incorporate **association loi 1901** (lowest-admin French legal
      entity; unlocks ADEME / Bpifrance / Fondation Free). Skip SAS —
      commercial structure is wrong for FOSS.
- [ ] Apply to ADEME circular-economy AAP (frame as e-waste mitigation,
      not security)
- [ ] Apply to Fondation Free digital-inclusion grant
- [ ] Apply to Shuttleworth Fellowship (rolling, high-reward long shot;
      use the origin-story narrative)
- [ ] Watch Mozilla Technology Fund annual theme announcement Q1 2027

### Non-phased / opportunistic

- **Reset Tech, Internet Society, OTF**: only if a specific call opens
  with a direct LETHE fit. Do not spec-write for these.
- **LFX Mentorship, EA Funds**: skip.

## Risks and mitigations

- **NLnet declines**: STF + Mobifree remain independent. Re-apply to
  NLnet in next cycle with a tightened scope.
- **Sovereign Tech Fund declines**: less severe — STF funds maintenance
  more than greenfield, so if LETHE hasn't shipped v1 yet it's a
  weaker fit. Defer to post-v1.1.
- **Incorporation overhead swallows dev time**: keep as individual
  until Phase 3; only incorporate when French lanes are worth the
  admin cost (i.e. when we have concrete ADEME/Bpifrance calls lined up).
- **Funder political risk (OTF)**: US State Dept funding has been
  volatile; do not plan budget around it.
- **Sponsor audience risk**: GitHub Sponsors revenue depends on the
  awesome-* release com plan working. If organic reach fails, drop
  sponsor target from €500/mo to €100/mo and do not over-rotate on it.
